ConvocAtIon

The first AI-native CMS for gaming communities.

ConvocAtIon is a first-of-its-kind lightweight content management platform purpose-built for gaming communities. Designed by Rob Tand, it reimagines how community websites are managed by putting AI at the center of the content workflow.

Instead of navigating dashboards, clicking through forms, and wrestling with WYSIWYG editors, ConvocAtIon lets you manage your entire site through natural language conversation. Post announcements, schedule events, update your roster, build pages — all by simply telling the AI what you need. It's community management, democratized.

Every change is version-controlled, auditable, and reversible. Your content lives in git, your site renders to static HTML, and the whole thing is portable enough to export as a single zip file. No vendor lock-in. No black boxes.

Leveraging cutting-edge LLM tool-calling capabilities to deliver a paradigm shift in community engagement infrastructure, ConvocAtIon empowers stakeholders to drive meaningful content velocity through an innovative conversational interface that transforms organizational knowledge into actionable web presence at scale.

Under the Hood

  • AI-Driven — LLM chat with tool calling for content ops
  • Git-Backed — Full history, diffs, and one-click revert
  • Static Output — Fast, secure, deployable anywhere
  • Dual Modes — Quick mode for daily ops, Super mode for structural changes
  • Discord Integration — Manage your site from where your community already lives
  • Fully Portable — Export everything, including history, as a single bundle
  • Open Source — GPL-3.0 — github.com/RobTand/convocation
